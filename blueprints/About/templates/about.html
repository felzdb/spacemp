Vision

To be the leading source of technological solutions for farmers, empowering them to face and overcome natural disasters, ensuring the safety and prosperity of their crops and families.

Mission

Provide farmers with access to real-time meteorological data and accurate forecasts through an easy-to-use platform, based on information provided by NASA. Our goal is to provide security and peace of mind to farmers, allowing them to enjoy life without constant worry about adverse weather changes.

Values

  • Innovation: We use the latest technologies to offer advanced and practical solutions.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to ensuring the safety and prosperity of farmers.
  • Transparency: We provide clear and accurate information to support decision-making.
  • Sustainability: We promote agricultural practices that protect and respect the environment.
  • Empathy: We understand the challenges faced by farmers and work to offer practical and effective support.
